Elements and their description.

FIRE! Fire is the element of power. The people of the land of Fire have the desire and will, energy and strength to achieve the desired goal...

EARTH! Earth is the element of matter. The inhabitants of the kingdom of Earth are inventive and strong, they are persistent and hardy...

AIR! Air is the element of freedom. The aerial nomads were freed from worldly worries and found peace and freedom. It is also obvious that they have a developed sense of humor!

WATER! Water is an element of change. The people of the Water tribe are able to adapt to many things, they have developed feelings of unity and love that help them stay together, no matter what...
